Bulking Diet

This diet focuses on gaining muscle mass. Includes high protein, carbs, and calorie-rich foods.

  • Oats with peanut butter
  • Chicken breast & rice
  • Whey protein shake
  • Sweet potatoes

Cutting Diet

A calorie-deficit diet to reduce fat while retaining muscle. Lean protein and low carbs.

  • Boiled eggs
  • Grilled fish
  • Steamed vegetables
  • Green tea

Keto Diet

Low-carb, high-fat diet to promote ketosis and burn fat for energy.

  • Avocado
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Butter coffee

Vegetarian Diet

Plant-based nutrition for strength and stamina, packed with protein and essential nutrients.

  • Lentils & legumes
  • Tofu stir-fry
  • Spinach smoothie
  • Paneer salad
